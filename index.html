Unlock your Skills with our Crevasse Rescue Courses!

During our 2-day intensive course, learn how to identify hazards, build anchors, and safely rescue a fallen climber. Our course combines modern crevasse rescue techniques and effective teaching styles to leave you feeling empowered to explore the mountains. Climbing Instruction: Unleash Your Inner Rock Climber!

Join us for a day of learning-focused climbing in the mountains. Our expert guides will teach you the ropes, from basic techniques to advanced skills, ensuring you gain the knowledge and confidence to apply those skills to your own recreational climbing in the future.

Crevasse Rescue 2-Day

Our crevasse rescue course is a must-have for any adventurer seeking to explore mountainous terrain safely. You will learn crucial skills to save yourself or a team member if they fall into a crevasse. Our experienced guides will teach you rope and anchor systems, pulley systems, and other specialized techniques that may mean the difference between life and death in a high-altitude emergency. You will practice these skills in a simulated environment, receiving feedback and personalized attention from our instructors.

Access the Alpine's 5-day mountaineering course is a comprehensive introduction to the world of mountaineering. Over the course of five days, you'll explore the stunning glaciers of the North Cascades and learn essential techniques for glacier travel, crevasse rescue, winter camping, and route-finding. You'll be guided by experienced instructors who will teach you the skills needed to safely navigate the mountain.
